Site Plan

Main Site Purpose

The main purpose of this site is to be the main page for a computer hardware manufacturing company. Here users will be able to view the kinds of products that this company produces and any offers that the company has.

Scenarios

Where will viewers be able to see the products that the company offers?

They will be able to navigate to a products page were a short list of main products will be listed. There, viewers will be able to see the spesifications for each product along with the pricing.

How can users buy a product from this company?

Users will be able to navigate to a buy page where a form will be displayed. There, users will be able to fill out their information along with a product of their choice. The webpage will remember previous orders that the user has placed at this webpage.
